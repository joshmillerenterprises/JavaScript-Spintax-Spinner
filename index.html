Spintax Article Spinner

Instructions

This application helps you to spin text that contains spintax.

Spintax come in the form of "{ text | text2 | textn... }" and then randomly selects from the options you've put.

For example, if you were to input

{Hi|Hello} {my name is|I am called} {Josh|Josh Miller}

The spinner will generate various variants of this sentence on a random basis every time.
